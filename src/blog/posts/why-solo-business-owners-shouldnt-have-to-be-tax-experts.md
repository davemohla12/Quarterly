## The System Assumes You’re an Accountant

If you’re a solo entrepreneur, freelancer, or creator, you’ve probably realized something frustrating:

The tax system was **not built for people like you**.

It assumes you have time to:

- Learn complex tax rules  
- Keep track of changing laws  
- Calculate federal and state taxes  
- File multiple forms throughout the year  
- Avoid penalties on your own

And all while trying to run your business.

## You Already Wear Enough Hats

As a solo business owner, you’re:

- CEO  
- Product designer  
- Customer support  
- Marketer  
- Bookkeeper

You don’t also want to be the **in-house tax specialist**.

✅ You didn’t go into business to read IRS publications.  
✅ You don’t have time to decipher Form 1040-ES.  
✅ You just want to know what to pay — and pay it on time.

## What’s at Risk if You Get It Wrong

Without help, it’s easy to:

- Underpay or overpay your quarterly taxes

- File the wrong forms  
- Pay too much to an accountant for something simple

And those mistakes can cost you real money — or worse, stress and wasted time you can’t afford.
