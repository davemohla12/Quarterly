## Why Accurate Tax Estimates Matter

Quarterly taxes can feel like a guessing game — but guess wrong, and you could end up:

- Owing penalties to the IRS  
- Overpaying and giving the government an interest-free loan  
- Stressing every time income fluctuates

Zenguider was built to fix that.

## Step 1: You Enter Your Income

Zenguider starts with the most important piece: **your income**.

✅ Whether it’s freelancing, consulting, content creation, or digital products — you enter how much you’ve earned so far this year.

We support:

- One-time payments  
- Monthly income  
- Variable income over time

## Step 2: We Subtract Your Business Expenses

Next, we estimate or let you enter your **deductible business expenses**:

- Software and tools  
- Office expenses  
- Travel and meals  
- Health insurance premiums  
- Home office deduction

✅ This gives us your **net profit** — the amount that’s actually taxable.

## Step 3: We Apply the Latest Tax Rates

Zenguider instantly applies the **latest federal rates**, including:

- Self-employment tax (15.3%)  
- Income tax based on your bracket (10–24% for most freelancers)  
- Standard deduction

✅ State tax? We’ve got that too. If your state requires quarterly taxes, we calculate that based on your income and sourcing.

## Step 4: Safe Harbor Rules Are Built In

Zenguider automatically checks:

- If you’re on track to meet the **IRS safe harbor rule**  
- Whether you’re paying **90% of your current year’s tax** or **100% of last year’s**  
- If you’ll avoid penalties even if you underpay slightly

✅ No guesswork — we tell you if you’re covered or need to pay more.

## Step 5: We Break It Down Quarterly

We take your estimated annual tax and:

- Divide it by 4  
- Adjust it if your income is rising or falling  
- Show you exactly what to pay for this quarter

✅ No spreadsheets. Just a clear number and a deadline.

## Step 6: You Get Clear Reminders

Before each deadline, we’ll:

- Remind you when it’s time to pay  
- Show you what’s due  
- Link you directly to IRS and state payment portals

✅ We even adjust your estimates if you update your income.
