## Why Quarterly Taxes Feel So Overwhelming

Here’s what the IRS expects you to do four times a year:

- Estimate your income (even when it fluctuates)  
- Subtract all your deductions  
- Calculate self-employment tax  
- Apply the correct federal income tax brackets  
- Consider your state’s tax rules  
- Divide your total into four payments  
- File the right forms  
- Pay by the exact deadlines

✅ And if you mess up? You could face penalties, interest, or a nasty surprise in April.

It’s not you.  
It’s the system.
