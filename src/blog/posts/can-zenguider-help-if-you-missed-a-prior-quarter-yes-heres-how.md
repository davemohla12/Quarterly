Life happens. Whether you forgot, didn’t realize you owed, or just got busy, it’s common to miss a quarterly tax payment.

## What Happens When You Miss a Quarter

✅ The IRS charges:

- A **late payment penalty** (~0.5% per month)  
- **Interest** on the unpaid amount  
- Possibly an **underpayment penalty** at year-end

But those penalties **stop growing** as soon as you pay what you owe.
