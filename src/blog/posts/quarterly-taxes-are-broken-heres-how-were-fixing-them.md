## Quarterly Taxes Weren’t Built for You

The quarterly tax system was created **decades ago**, designed for businesses with full-time accountants — not modern freelancers and solo entrepreneurs.

Today’s self-employed workers are:

- Juggling multiple income streams  
- Working across state lines  
- Managing unpredictable cash flow

Yet the IRS still expects them to manually calculate and pay estimated taxes four times a year, on their own.

## What’s Wrong With the Current System

Let’s be honest: it’s a mess.

❌ Confusing rules  
❌ Vague IRS forms (hello, Form 1040-ES)  
❌ No real-time guidance  
❌ No built-in reminders  
❌ No centralized place to manage payments  

And if you get it wrong? You risk **penalties, interest, and IRS notices** — all because the system wasn’t made for people like you.

## Why It’s So Hard to Get Right

You have to:

- Estimate your **net profit**  
- Calculate **self-employment + income taxes**  
- Understand **state vs. federal** rules  
- Keep track of **quarterly deadlines**  
- Make payments through **separate IRS systems**

That’s a lot for anyone — let alone someone running a business solo.
