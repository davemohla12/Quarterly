## Freedom Comes With Responsibility

When you work for yourself, you get the freedom to:

- Set your own hours  
- Choose your clients  
- Work from anywhere  
- Build something that’s yours

But with that freedom comes a new responsibility — taxes.

You no longer have an employer handling withholdings.  
You are the employer.  
And that means **quarterly taxes**.

## Why Quarterly Taxes Feel Like a Full-Time Job

The IRS expects you to:

- Predict your income (even if it changes month to month)  
- Deduct your business expenses  
- Calculate self-employment + income tax  
- Know your state’s tax rules  
- Make four payments per year  
- Avoid penalties and interest

✅ It’s not just paying — it’s tracking, estimating, filing, and remembering.

And that’s before you even get to April.
